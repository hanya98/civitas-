ABOUT CIVITAS

Civitas is a centralized digital platform designed to enable citizens to lodge grievances, track public development projects, and engage directly with authorities. The system connects various departments to ensure transparency and accountability.

Issues not taken up for redress:

  • Court related / Subjudice matters
  • Religious matters
  • Service matters of government employees
Note:
  1. Escalation mechanisms are available for unresolved grievances.
  2. No fee is charged for filing grievances on this platform.
