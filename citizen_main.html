About Civitas

Civitas is a unified digital platform designed to make city services more accessible, transparent and responsive for citizens. Through this portal, you can quickly report civic issues, monitor their resolution status and stay informed about important developments happening in your area.

The platform helps bridge the gap between citizens and city administration by ensuring that grievances are tracked efficiently and addressed in a timely manner. You can also explore ongoing infrastructure projects, government policies, public welfare schemes and participate in surveys to share your valuable feedback.

  • Report civic problems anytime, from anywhere
  • Track complaint progress in real-time
  • Discover development work near your location
  • Access government policies and welfare schemes
  • Share suggestions to improve city services

Together, we can build a cleaner, safer and more efficient city for everyone.
